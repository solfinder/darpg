Trend Help

This report provides a trend analysis of the number of incoming grievances and disposals over a period of time. The reports is organized by department which can be selceted from the combo box at the top and it shows the pattern of workload which is arriving within the department as also the resolution efficiency. It also gives an idea about the piling up of pending grievances over a period of time. Once the trend is known, further investigation can be carried out to determine the root cause of the characteristics and to optimize the operations accordingy.
